Frequently Asked Questions

What items can I donate?

We accept all clean, gently used clothing items including shirts, pants, dresses, jackets, and accessories. Items should be in good condition without significant damage. We do not accept undergarments, swimwear, or heavily worn items.

How do I receive my rewards?

After placing your items in the bin, scan the QR code on the bin with your smartphone. You'll be directed to our rewards portal where you can choose your reward option and it will be instantly added to your account.

What happens to the items I donate?

All items go through our sustainability process:

  • Quality items are cleaned and made available for purchase at affordable prices
  • Items in good condition but not suitable for resale are donated to local charities
  • Damaged items are responsibly recycled into new materials
Can I donate at locations other than airports?

Currently, our donation bins are only available at participating airports. However, we're expanding to train stations and cruise terminals soon. Check back for updates on new locations!

Is there a limit to how many items I can donate?

No, there's no limit! You can donate as many items as you'd like. Each item qualifies for rewards, so more donations mean more benefits for you and the planet.
