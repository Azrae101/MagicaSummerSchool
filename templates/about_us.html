About SustainABag

Travel light. Give back. Shop sustainably.

Did you know? According to the Fall 2024 Global Rescue Traveler Sentiment and Safety Survey, 32% of travelers admitted that overpacking was their top travel mistake in 2024. Overpacking leads to excess baggage fees, makes it harder to navigate airports, and adds frustration to your journey. By packing lighter, you can avoid these hassles and travel more sustainably.
What happens when travelers overpack? About 58% of travelers who exceed their luggage allowance choose to pay for overweight baggage fees, while the remaining 42% opt to leave items behind to reduce weight. Source: Travel Agent Central, Newsdesk

Our Mission

"To revolutionize travel by reducing waste and promoting sustainable practices through a circular economy for travel essentials."

SustainABag is a green travel platform dedicated to reducing overconsumption and promoting sustainability among travelers. We believe that traveling light not only makes your journey easier, but also helps the planet by reducing waste and carbon footprint.

Our Concept

If you find yourself overpacked or with extra clothing during your travels, SustainABag is here to help. Simply drop off your unused clothes at our store. Donate them or sell them at a low price. We carefully check all items for quality: those in great condition are resold to other travelers at affordable prices, while the rest are donated to local charities.

Why We Do It

  • Reduce textile waste and promote a circular economy
  • Support local communities through donations
  • Make sustainable travel easy and accessible for everyone
  • Reduce carbon footprint from textile production
  • Create a community of conscious travelers

Our Impact

Since our founding, we've diverted over 5,000 pounds of clothing from landfills and helped thousands of travelers lighten their load. Our community of eco-conscious travelers continues to grow, creating a positive ripple effect in the travel industry.
